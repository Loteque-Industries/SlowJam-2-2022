          noir
          /nwär/
          Learn to pronounce
          noun
          noun: noir
          1. a genre of crime film or fiction characterized by cynicism, fatalism, and moral ambiguity.
            "his film proved that a Brit could do noir as darkly as any American"
            • a film or novel in the noir genre.
              plural noun: noirs
              "he says he's making a noir"
          Origin
          1970s: from film noir.
